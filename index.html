About Coove Coffees

Coove Coffees is proudly rooted in the heart of Chikkamagaluru. Started by a passionate village entrepreneur, our journey is built on dedication, tradition, and love for coffee.

We specialize in carefully roasted coffee beans and natural coffee powder, crafted with precision to deliver rich aroma and authentic taste. Every batch is roasted with care, ensuring quality, freshness, and true flavor in every cup.

From farm to cup, we bring you honest coffee made with passion and purity.
